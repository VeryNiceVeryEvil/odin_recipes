Dulce de Leche Fudge

Siona Watson, CC BY 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

This is a simple recipe that can be made in the microwave

Ingredients
  • white chocolate chips
  • store bought or homemade dulce de leche
  • unsalted butter
  • vanilla extract
  • salt
  • pecans

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients and line an 8x8 pan with aluminum foil and cooking spray
  2. In microwave safe bowl, combine white chocolate, dulce de leche, and butter
  3. Microwave in 30 second increments, stirring. Continue until smooth
  4. Stir in vanilla and salt, then pecans
  5. Scrape into pan and refridgerate to set for at least 2 hours
  6. Once set, chop into 1 inch squares
